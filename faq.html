Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about TechTical, our coverage, or how to get involved? Start here! We’re always updating our FAQ based on reader feedback and your most common questions.

General

How often is TechTical updated?
We publish new stories, reviews, and analysis every weekday. Our front page highlights the latest breakthroughs and the most relevant tech trends as soon as they happen.
What topics do you cover?
We focus on AI, science, gadgets, green tech, digital culture, security, startups, and the future of work. We also feature interviews with innovators and practical guides for tech enthusiasts, students, and professionals.
Is your content free?
Yes! All of our articles, guides, and reviews are free to read. You can support us by subscribing to our newsletter or sharing our work on social media.
How do I subscribe to the TechTical newsletter?
You can subscribe using the form at the bottom of any page. We send regular updates, exclusive content, and tips straight to your inbox. You can unsubscribe at any time.
Can I contribute or write for TechTical?
Absolutely! We welcome pitches and guest articles from diverse voices. Visit our Contact page to share your interest, portfolio, or story idea.

Community & Features

How can I join TechTical’s community?
Join discussions in the comments, follow us on social media, and subscribe to our newsletter. We host regular AMAs, webinars, and meetups for our readers and contributors.
Do you review products and services?
Yes. Our team and guest reviewers test and review new gadgets, apps, platforms, and online services with an emphasis on honesty, accessibility, and real-world use.
How do you choose what to review or report?
We combine editorial insight, trending topics, and reader requests. We value unbiased, independent coverage and never accept payment for reviews or features.
How do you ensure your reporting is accurate?
We fact-check every story, consult experts, and link to primary sources whenever possible. Corrections are promptly made and clearly noted on the page.
Is my data safe when I use TechTical?
We respect your privacy. We never sell your information or track you beyond basic analytics. See our detailed Privacy Policy for more.

Contact & Support

How can I get in touch with TechTical?
Use our Contact page for all inquiries, feedback, or to report an issue. Our team usually responds within 48 hours.
Can I advertise or partner with TechTical?
Yes! We welcome ethical advertising and partnerships that align with our mission. Please reach out with your proposal through our contact page.
